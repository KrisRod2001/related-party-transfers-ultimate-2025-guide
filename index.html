Why Related Party Transfers Are Booming in 2025

Families and businesses are moving billions of dollars worth of property between spouses, children, trusts, and companies — almost entirely stamp-duty free.

Reasons:

  • Refinancing (banks often require property in one name)
  • Divorce & separation settlements
  • Estate & succession planning
  • Asset protection (moving property into trusts)
  • Aged care & Centrelink planning

Who Qualifies for Zero or Nominal Duty in 2025

100% stamp duty exempt (all states & territories):
• Married spouses ↔ spouses
• De facto partners (2+ years or registered)
• Domestic partners (VIC, NSW, ACT registered relationships)

Your Free 2025 Related Party Transfer Checklist

Download or screenshot this:

  • Marriage certificate OR statutory declaration of de facto relationship
  • Copy of trust deed (if transferring to/from a trust)
  • Company ASIC extract (if company involved)
  • 100-point ID for all parties
  • Original Certificate of Title (or consent to act without)
  • Transfer form (NOT a standard contract)
  • Purchaser/Transferee Declaration with correct exemption code
  • Council rates notice (for adjustment)
  • Mortgagee consent letter (if applicable)
